Lesson 3 Highlights

Julia McLane

Topic 1

Hypertext Transfer Protocol is possibly one of the most useful unseen force of the internet. Without it, most users wouldn’t be able to conveniently access information from other clients and servers. HTTP is the language or protocol that allows web browsers and web servers to communicate over the internet.

Topic 2

Some say that it is the little acts of kindness that make the biggest impacts. While we don’t have the advanced technology available to give computers the kind of artificial intelligence that will enable them give little acts of kindness, some humans have enacted a “kindness” and made it possible for the Domain Name System to exist.

Topic 3

Perhaps one of the main focal points of technology is the idea that all devices should be able to communicate with each other. This can mean a wide range of topics, like how social media brings together a form of social communication or how data exchange technology like Bluetooth. However, most businesses and companies need their computers to share information with each other. While most companies will use a client-server, networks like a peer-to-peer network work on a smaller scale.

Topic 4

Data Packets are like the building blocks of the internet. They are essentially the life source for all data transfer on the internet. Without data packets, I image the internet would become even more complex than it already is.

Topic 5

If there was ever an iconic duo in history, it would probably be the Internet and websites. Often these two terms go hand and hand, as they often complement each other. Websites offer the user into the world of the Internet and all of the services it provides.
